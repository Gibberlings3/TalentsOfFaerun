Shapechange Forms

These are the forms available to a wizard or sorcerer casting Shapechange.

Giant Troll

Strength: 22
Dexterity: 18

Base Armor Class: 4
Number of Attacks: 3
Base THAC0: 8
Attack Damage: 2d6 (piercing)

Special Abilities:
- Immune to stun, sleep, hold, paralysis
- Regenerates 5 Hit Points per second
- Can cast wizard spells in this form

Greater Basilisk

Strength: 18
Dexterity: 10

Base Armor Class: 2
Number of Attacks: 3
Base THAC0: 11
Damage: 1d8 piercing +poison (3 hp damage per second for 6 seconds)

Special Abilities:
- Petrifying gaze (target must save vs. petrification at -4 or be turned to stone permanently) once per round

Invisible Stalker

Strength: 18/10
Dexterity: 17

Base Armor Class: -3
Number of Attacks: 1
Base THAC0: 11
Atack Damage: 2d8 crushing

Special Abilities:
- Automatically becomes Improved Invisible once per round (unless suppressed by True Seeing, Glitterdust or similar)
- Immunity to poison and disease
- Magic Resistance: 30%
- Fire Resistance: 30%
- Cold Resistance: 30%
- Acid Resistance: 30%
- Electricity Resistance: 30%
- Missile Resistance: 30%
- Can cast wizard spells in this form

Iron Golem

Strength: 24
Dexterity: 9

Base Armor Class: 3
Number of Attacks: 1
Base THAC0: 3
Attack Damage: 4d10 (crushing)

Special Abilities:
- Fire Resistance: 125%
- Cold Resistance: 100%
- Electrical Resistance: 100%
- Acid Resistance: 100%
- Magic Resistance: 100% (cannot be lowered)
- Magic Fire Resistance: 125%
- Magic Cold Resistance: 100%
- Slashing Resistance: 20%
- Crushing Resistance: 20%
- Piercing Resistance: 20%
- Missile Resistance: 20%
- Immune to backstab, weapons of +2 enchantment or less, sleep, fear, hold, charm, paralysis, poison.
- Cannot cast wizard spells in this form

Marilith

Strength: 19
Dexterity: 18

Base Armor Class: -6
Number of Attacks: 5
Base THAC0: 6
Attack Damage: 1d8, plus additional fire, acid, cold, electricity, magic, or poison damage, at random

Special Abilities:
- Teleport, at will
- Immunity to charm, fear, poison, and disease
- Magic resistance: 85%
- Fire resistance: 100%
- Electricity resistance: 100%
- Cold resistance: 50%
- Can cast wizard spells in this form

Mind Flayer

Strength: 10
Dexterity: 10

Base Armor Class: 5
Number of Attacks: 4
Base THAC0: 11
Attack Damage: 1d2 (crushing), -5 Intelligence

Special Abilities:
- 90% magic resistance
- Psionic Blast at will
- Can cast wizard spells in this form
