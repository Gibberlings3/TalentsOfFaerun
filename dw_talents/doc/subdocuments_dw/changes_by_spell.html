Stratagems of Mystra: Changes by Spell

This page lists all the changes made to each spell, organized by by spell type (priest/wizard), then by level, and finally in alphabetical order. Each change notes which games are affected and includes a link back to the tweak that made that change. If you would prefer to see the spell changes organized by category, you can do so here.

Level 1 priest

Cause Light Wounds

The spell now does 5 hit points damage, plus 1 per level to a maximum of 5. (not Spell Revisions; source)

Cure Light Wounds

The spell now heals 5 hit points, plus 1 per level to a maximum of 5. (not Spell Revisions; source)

Entangle

Entangle does not affect very large creatures like giants or dragons (which brush the entwining plants aside), incorporeal creatures like shadows or wraiths (which pass through them), amorphous creatures like slimes or elementals (which flow around them), or creatures shrouded in magical fire (which burn them away in their vicinity). (source)

Magical Stone

Magical Stone does 3d4 damage (6d4 vs undead), rather than 1d4. (BG1TUTU,BG2EE,BGEE,BGT,EET,SoA,ToB; not Spell Revisions; source)

Level 2 priest

Cause Moderate Wounds

The spell now does 10 hit points damage, plus 1 per level to a maximum of 10. (not Spell Revisions; source)

Chant

The spell no longer prevents spellcasting (IWDEE; source)

Cure Moderate Wounds

The spell now heals 10 hit points, plus 1 per level to a maximum of 10. (not Spell Revisions; source)

Flame Blade

The spell now does +1d4 fire damage rather than +2 (IWDEE; source)

Slow Poison

The spell now genuinely slows the effect of poison (reducing the speed of its effect by 90%) instead of neutralising it altogether. (BG1TUTU,BG2EE,BGEE,BGT,EET,SoA,ToB; not Spell Revisions; source)

Level 3 priest

Call Lightning

The lightning bolts now strike somewhat more frequently (BG1TUTU,BG2EE,BGEE,BGT,EET,SoA,ToB; not Spell Revisions; source)

Cause Medium Wounds

The spell now does 15 hit points damage, plus 1 per level to a maximum of 15. (not Spell Revisions; source)

Circle of Bones

The spell's duration is increased to 1 round per level and it no longer immobilizes the caster. (source)

Cure Medium Wounds

The spell now heals 15 hit points, plus 1 per level to a maximum of 15. (not Spell Revisions; source)

Holy Smite

The spell now does 1d4 damage per level, rather than 1d6 per two levels with a cap at 5d6. (IWDEE; source)

Protection From Fire

The spell now lasts for 1 turn per level rather than 3 rounds + 1 round per level. (BG1TUTU,BG2EE,BGEE,BGT,EET,SoA,ToB; not Spell Revisions; source)

Strength of One

Strength of One increases recipients' strength to 18/76 rather than 18/75. (BG1TUTU,BG2EE,BGEE,BGT,EET,SoA,ToB; not Spell Revisions; source)

Summon Insects

Insects act as summoned creatures: magic resistance or Dispel Magic do not affect them; they inflict piercing damage; they cannot affect creatures unaffected by nonmagical weapons; they will not attack undead; they cannot penetrate the extreme heat or cold of a Fire Shield. (not Spell Revisions; source)

Unholy Blight

The spell now does 1d4 damage per level, rather than 1d6 per two levels with a cap at 5d6. (IWDEE; source)

Level 4 priest

Cure Serious Wounds

The spell now heals 20 hit points, plus 1 per level to a maximum of 20. (not Spell Revisions; source)

Defensive Harmony

The spell increases AC by +4 instead of +2 (BG1TUTU,BG2EE,BGEE,BGT,EET,SoA,ToB; not Spell Revisions; source)

Lesser Restoration

Temporarily lost ability score points are now restored. (source)

Thorn Spray

Damage is increased from 2d10 to 4d12, increasing to 5d12 at 9th level and a maximum of 6d12 at 11th level. (source)

Level 5 priest

Divine Protection

The spell has been renamed from 'Shield of Lathander' and is now usable by clerics of all alignments; it is self-only and has a shortened casting time. (source)

Flame Strike

The spell does 1d8 damage per level, not a flat 8d6 (IWDEE; source)

The spell is targeted on a creature rather than a point (IWDEE; source)

Insect Plague

Insects act as summoned creatures: magic resistance or Dispel Magic do not affect them; they inflict piercing damage; they cannot affect creatures unaffected by nonmagical weapons; they will not attack undead; they cannot penetrate the extreme heat or cold of a Fire Shield. (not Spell Revisions; source)

Slay Living

The spell no longer requires a successful melee attack. (BG1TUTU,BG2EE,BGEE,BGT,EET,SoA,ToB; not Spell Revisions; source)

True Seeing

Truesight removes the effect of Blindness. (not Spell Revisions; source)

Level 6 priest

Blade Barrier

The blades only affect hostile creatures. (not Spell Revisions; source)

A successful saving throw now reduces damage by half rather than negating it altogether. (BG1TUTU,BG2EE,BGEE,BGT,EET,SoA,ToB; not Spell Revisions; source)

Harm

The spell inflicts 150 hit points of damage, but leaves its target with at least 1 hit point. (not Spell Revisions; source)

Sol's Searing Orb

Sol's Searing Orb does 6d6 damage, not 3d6, and does not require a hit roll. (BG1TUTU,BG2EE,BGEE,BGT,EET,SoA,ToB; not Spell Revisions; source)

Healing Mist

Impervious Sanctity of Mind

The spell also includes a Spell Shield effect (first anti-magic spell absorbed harmlessly) and grant immunity to spell failure (though spells can still be disrupted by damage). (source)

Nature's Beauty

Anti-illusion magic will not remove the blindness effect (or, on Spell Revisions installs, the Charm effect). (source)

The spell is moved to the Enchantment school. (not Spell Revisions; source)

The symbol only affects hostile creatures. (not Spell Revisions; source)

Level 1 wizard

Chromatic Orb

The spell now works as in Icewind Dale: less drastic effects at higher levels, but no bonus to the saving throw. (BG1TUTU,BG2EE,BGEE,BGT,EET,SoA,ToB; not Spell Revisions; source)

Color Spray

The spell now affects any creature of the caster's level or below. (BG1TUTU,BG2EE,BGEE,BGT,EET,SoA,ToB; not Spell Revisions; source)

Larloch's Minor Drain

The spell drains 1d4 hit points at 1st level, increasing by 1d4 every 2 levels to a maximum of 5d4. (not Spell Revisions; source)

Level 2 wizard

Level 3 wizard

Ghost Armor

The spell gives an AC of 1 (rather than 2) and lasts for 1 hour rather than 1 round per level. (IWDEE; source)

Haste

Haste is cancelled by Slow. (BG2EE,BGEE,EET,IWDEE; not Spell Revisions; source)

Lightning Bolt

Any creature is only damaged once by each Lightning Bolt, no matter how often it rebounds into them. (BG2EE,BGEE,EET,IWDEE; not Spell Revisions; source)

Melf's Minute Meteors

Meteors count as +2 weapons (rather than +6). (not Spell Revisions; source)

Protection From Normal Missiles

The spell blocks most magical arrows, bolts etc that lack a formal "plus": that is, it blocks fire arrows, ice arrows and the like. The exceptions are the Arrows of Detonation, the Arrows of Slaying, and the Arrows of Dispelling. (not Spell Revisions; source)

Skull Trap

Damage is capped at 12d6. (not Spell Revisions; source)

Slow

Slow is cancelled by Haste. (BG2EE,BGEE,EET,IWDEE; not Spell Revisions; source)

Spell Thrust

This spell may be used to remove magical protections even from creatures which could not normally be targetted by spells because they are invisible. (source)

Acid Storm

The victim initially suffers 10d6 points of acid damage. After the initial damage, the victim must save vs. Spell to avoid more damage. If unsuccessful, the victim continues to suffer acid damage in the following rounds, sustaining two fewer dice of damage each round: that is, 8d6 damage with this spell on the first round, 6d6 on the second round, 4d6 on the third round, 2d6 on the fourth round, and the spell ends on the fifth round. Moving out of the area of effect does not stop the damage. (source)
