Polymorph Self Forms

These are the forms available to a wizard, sorcerer, or bard casting Polymorph Self.

Hell Hound

Strength: 18
Dexterity: 15

Base Armor Class: 4
Number of Attacks: 1
Attack Damage: 1d10 piercing + 1d6 fire

Special Abilities:
- Immune to fire damage
- +50% cold damage
- Breathe a cone of flame once per 3 rounds. Creatures caught in the cone take 3d6 fire damage (save vs. breath for 1/2)

Hill Giant Barbarian

Strength: 19
Dexterity: 10

Base Armor Class: 3
Number of Attacks: 1
Attack Damage: 2d8 piercing (attacks as +1 weapon)

Special Abilities:
- Barbarian Rage 1/day

Ogre Mage

Strength: 18/00
Dexterity: 12

Base Armor Class: 4
Number of Attacks: 1
Attack Damage: 1d10 slashing (attacks as +1 weapon)

Special Abilities:
- Can cast spells in this form

Rat

Strength: 10
Dexterity: 20

Base Armor Class: 4
Number of Attacks: 1
Attack Damage: 1d2 piercing

Special Abilities:
- Hide, at will (cannot be used in combat)

Spider

Strength: 15
Dexterity: 17

Base Armor Class: 4
Number of Attacks: 2
Attack Damage: 1d4 (piercing), strikes as a +1 weapon, poison (1 damage per 2 seconds for 5 rounds; Save vs. Poison at +1 negates)

Special Abilities:
- Immunity to web
- Increased movement rate
- Spit a web tangle once per 2 rds at a single target (must save vs. breath or be webbed for 2 rds)
